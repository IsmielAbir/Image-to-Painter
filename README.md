# 🎨 Image to Painter

Transform your images into beautiful artistic styles with a single click!
---

## ✨ Features
### 🎨 Art Styles
- ✏️ **Pencil Sketch**  
  Classic black & white sketch effect
- 🎨 **Color Sketch**  
  Sketch with original colors preserved
- 🧸 **Cartoon Effect**  
  Transform images into cartoon-style visuals
- 🌊 **Watercolor**  
  Soft and artistic watercolor painting effect
- 🖌️ **Oil Painting**  
  Rich, textured oil painting style
- 🖍️ **Crayon Effect**  
  Child-like crayon drawing effect
- 📚 **Comic Book**  
  Bold and dramatic comic-style visuals
-  **Pop Art**  
  Vibrant and colorful pop art transformation
